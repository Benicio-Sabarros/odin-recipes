Cookies Recipe

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes dozens of delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles loaded with chocolate chips!


Making bakery-worthy chocolate chip cookies is much easier than it seems. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopeed walnuts

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature

  2. Step 2: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.

  3. Step 3: Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.

  4. Step 4: Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.

  5. Step 5: Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.

  6. Step 6: Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.

  7. Step 7: Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.

  8. Step 8: Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Step 9: Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
